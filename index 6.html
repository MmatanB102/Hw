One Bowl Chocolate Cake.

It only takes a few minutes to prepare the batter for this rich and moist one bowl Chocolate Cake .And if you want you can Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups Of White Sugar

  • 2 Cups Of All-Purpose flour

  • 1 Cup Of Unsweetened Cocoa powder

  • 1.5 Tbsp Baking Powder

  • 1.5 Tbsp Baking Soda

  • 1 Tbsp Salt

  • 2 Eggs

  • 1 Cup Of Milk

  • 0.5 Cup Of Vegetable oil

  • 2 Tbsp Of Vanilla extract

  • 1 Cup Boiling Of Water

    How to Make One-Bowl Chocolate Cake

    It truly couldn't be easier to make this chocolate cake â€” just mix the ingredients and bake!

    You'll find the full recipe below, but here's a brief overview of what you can expect:

    Mix Dry Ingredients.

  1. Stir or whisk together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until evenly combined.

    2. Add Wet Ingredients.

  2. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla.
  3. Mix on medium speed for about two minutes.
  4. Stir in boiling water.(Don't worry if the batter seems a little thin, as reviewers say that's perfectly normal)

    5. Bake.

  5. Pour the cake batter into prepared pans.
  6. Bake for about half an hour, until a toothpick can come out clean when inserted in the middle.
  7. Let cool and top with your favorite frosting.
